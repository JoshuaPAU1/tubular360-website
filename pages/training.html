Training

Safety Training

At Tubular 360, we prioritize safety in all our operations. Our safety training programs are designed to equip our team with the knowledge and skills needed to work safely and effectively in the oil and gas industry. From hazard recognition to emergency response, we cover all aspects of safety to ensure a secure working environment.

Technical Training

Our technical training programs are tailored to meet the specific needs of the oil and gas industry. We offer hands-on training in the use and maintenance of oil tools, inspection techniques, and industry best practices. Our experienced trainers ensure that participants gain practical skills that can be directly applied in the field.

Customized Training Solutions

We understand that every organization has unique training needs. That’s why we offer customized training solutions that can be tailored to your specific requirements. Whether you need on-site training, virtual sessions, or a combination of both, we work with you to develop a training program that meets your goals and enhances your team’s capabilities.

Specialized Technical Training

Tubular 360 offers a range of practical, industry-relevant training designed to build technical competence and field readiness. These programs focus on hands-on learning, safety, and adherence to best practices across oilfield operations.

  • Premium Connection & Proprietary Thread Training – Proper handling, make-up, torque application, and maintenance of premium connections.
  • NDT & OCTG Inspection Techniques – Introduction to Magnetic Particle, Ultrasonic, and Visual Testing methods for tubular inspection.
  • Rig-Ready Preparation & Handling – Best practices for preparing, protecting, and deploying tubulars for rig operations.
  • Material & Dimensional Inspection Awareness – Understanding gauging tools, dimensional tolerances, and visual inspection criteria.
  • Surface Treatment & Corrosion Control – Application and maintenance of protective coatings and thread treatments.
  • Technical Support & Troubleshooting – Identifying and resolving field-level issues during tool operations and maintenance.
