Our Services

Oil Tools

At Tubular 360, we buy and sell high-quality oil tools for the oil and gas industry. From drill bits to completion equipment, our products are sourced from trusted manufacturers and tested for reliability. Whether you’re purchasing a single tool or a complete set, we ensure quality, value, and expert support for every transaction.

Inspection Services

Our inspection services are designed to ensure the integrity and safety of your oil and gas operations. We offer a range of inspection solutions, including non-destructive testing (NDT), visual inspections, and equipment audits. Our certified inspectors use advanced technology to help you maintain compliance and prevent costly downtime.

Training Programs

We provide comprehensive training programs tailored to the needs of the oil and gas industry. Our courses cover safety protocols, equipment handling, and industry best practices. Led by experienced professionals, our training helps teams operate efficiently and safely in demanding environments.

Offshore Services

Tubular 360 provides a wide range of offshore services, including inspection, equipment handling, and logistics support for offshore drilling and production operations. Our offshore crews are trained to work safely and efficiently in challenging marine environments, ensuring that every operation meets the highest industry standards.

Yard Management

Our yard management services provide full control over your tubular inventory and logistics. From material tracking and handling to inspection coordination and dispatching, we streamline yard operations to optimize efficiency, minimize downtime, and maintain full traceability of your assets.

Purchasing

We facilitate the purchase of high-quality oilfield tools and equipment for a wide range of applications. Leveraging our network of trusted suppliers, we source reliable products at competitive prices. Our purchasing support ensures that clients receive equipment tailored to their operational requirements — quickly, efficiently, and cost-effectively.
